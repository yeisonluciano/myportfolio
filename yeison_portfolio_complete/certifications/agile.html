Agile Project Management

LinkedIn Learning • Endorsed by IIBA®

Through the Agile Foundations course by LinkedIn Learning, endorsed by the International Institute of Business Analysis (IIBA®), I gained a solid understanding of Agile principles, frameworks and project management techniques. The course emphasized iterative development, team collaboration and value‑driven delivery across various Agile methodologies.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • Agile Mindset & Principles – Learned the core values and principles of the Agile Manifesto, focusing on adaptability, customer collaboration and continuous improvement.
  • Agile Methodologies – Explored popular frameworks including Scrum, Kanban and Lean, understanding their workflows, roles and ceremonies.
  • Agile Project Management – Developed skills in managing Agile projects through iterative cycles, backlog refinement, sprint planning and retrospectives.
  • Team Roles & Collaboration – Gained clarity on key Agile roles such as Product Owner, Scrum Master and Development Team, and how they interact to deliver value.
  • Continuous Feedback & Delivery – Emphasized the importance of stakeholder feedback, incremental releases and maintaining a sustainable pace of development.
  • Business Analysis Integration – Understood how Agile aligns with business analysis practices, especially in defining user stories, acceptance criteria and prioritizing features based on business value.
