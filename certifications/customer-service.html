Customer Service Foundations

LinkedIn Learning • PMI® Registered Education Provider

Through the Customer Service Foundations course by LinkedIn Learning, I developed essential skills for delivering empathetic, solution‑oriented support across diverse industries. The course is recognized by the Project Management Institute (PMI®) as part of its Registered Education Provider program, validating its relevance for professionals in client‑facing and project environments.

Key Learning Outcomes

  • Customer Support Fundamentals – Mastered the principles of effective customer service, including active listening, empathy and problem resolution.
  • Communication & Relationship Building – Learned techniques for building trust, handling difficult conversations and maintaining professionalism across channels.
  • Service Strategy & Process Awareness – Gained insight into service workflows, escalation procedures and aligning support with business goals.
  • Customer Retention & Satisfaction – Focused on delivering consistent value, managing expectations and turning feedback into actionable improvements.
  • Cross‑functional Collaboration – Understood how customer service integrates with project management, sales and operations to enhance the overall client experience.
